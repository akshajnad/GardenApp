Welcome to GardenApp

This is our extended "About Us" section, describing how GardenApp supports shelters by providing a platform to request fresh produce.

We connect local farmers or community gardens with shelters in need, helping them plan, plant, and harvest produce efficiently. Once you submit a request, our admin team can create a customized planting schedule.

Interested in registering? Please reach out to us directly so we can establish a relationship. We want to ensure that each shelter is onboarded properly.

After you're registered, you can log in and submit requests for produce, specifying how many people you serve, any special dietary needs, etc. We'll handle the rest!
